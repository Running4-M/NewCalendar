How to Create Events

You can add any event to your calendar, whether it's work, school, or home-related—no task is off-limits! To schedule your events for the week, simply click on the day you plan to start working on a specific task. This will bring up a form to guide you through creating the event.

In the form, you’ll need to:

  • Enter a title for your event.
  • Select the type of AI assistance you’d like, such as "Create Content" or any other available options.
  • Add a short description or go into more detail if needed. For example, you can use brief bullet points or ideas—perfect grammar is not necessary.

Once you’ve filled out the details, click Save Event. The event will be saved and scheduled for the selected day. For example, if you set the event for Thursday but today is Monday, the AI response will be generated on Thursday. This way, when you start working on your task, you already have a helpful starting point.

AI Responses

Once the AI generates a response, it will appear in the responses section. Here’s what you can expect:

  • The response will display the date when the event was scheduled, so you know when it originates from.
  • The event’s title will match the title in the response, ensuring clarity.
  • Clicking on the response will expand it, showing the AI-generated content in detail.

If you’d like to refine the response or add more details, click on the Start Chat feature. This allows you to engage in a chat with the AI to further customize the response based on your requirements. These responses are stored and won’t be deleted, so you can revisit them anytime.

Note: If you're just testing the calendar and adding an event, it may take a couple of minutes for the response to be shown. Please be patient as the AI processes the information.
